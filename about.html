About Us

Welcome to Tannu's Restaurant

Tannu Restaurant, located in the heart of Varanasi, is a well-loved and rapidly growing food destination run by the passionate and talented Mrs. Tannu. From the moment it opened, the restaurant has captured the hearts of the city's food lovers with its authentic taste, homely ambiance, and exceptional service. What truly sets Tannu Restaurant apart is Mrs. Tannu’s personal touch in every aspect — from selecting fresh ingredients daily to ensuring that every dish is prepared with love, care, and hygiene. The kitchen maintains the highest cleanliness standards, which is one of the main reasons customers feel safe and satisfied dining here.


The menu offers a wide range of traditional Indian dishes as well as local Banarasi favorites, each bursting with flavor and prepared using age-old recipes passed down through generations. Customers consistently praise the taste, presentation, and comforting feel of the meals, many describing it as “just like home-cooked food.” The restaurant is also known for its prompt service and polite staff, making every visit a pleasant experience. Whether it’s lunch with friends or a family dinner, Tannu Restaurant has become a top choice for many locals. Mrs. Tannu’s dedication and love for food truly shine in every bite.
